New Session Flow enhancements bring deeper insights into user journeys

on August 31, 2018

By Eddie Chow, InnoVi Chief Technology Officer

The Session Flow report, which was an innovation in the bot analytics industry
when Chatbase first launched, is one of our most popular features (available in
both Standard and Enterprise editions). So, we’re excited to announce improvements
to Session Flow that will help you analyze user journeys even more deeply and intuitively.

First, starting now, you can expose more branches (up to 24, including
up to 12 each for Handled and Not Handled states) for the paths/intents shown
in the report. This feature helps you dive more deeply into the journeys that
matter most to you, and gives you visibility into interactions that were
previously hidden in the “Other” bucket.

About Chatbase

Chatbase is a cloud service for designing, analyzing, and optimizing any
conversational experience. Our newest product, Chatbase Enterprise Edition
(available via Early Access Program), helps contact centers find hidden
insights about customer interactions in chat or call logs.
Powered by Google machine learning, this approach--far more accurate, comprehensive,
and efficient than guesswork--guides development of exactly the right AI-powered
virtual agent for meeting customer needs 24x7.
